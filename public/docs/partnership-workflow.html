1. System overview

The lifecycle of a TDI partnership

Every school moves through one pipeline: Sales (close the deal) to Leadership (manage the partnership) to Hub (educator engagement). Operations sits behind Leadership as the internal documentation layer. The principal sees their Leadership Dashboard. Your team sees everything.

1
Sales -- Lead comes in, opportunity tracked, demos, proposal, contract signed
↓
2
Deal signed (automation) -- One click creates partnership, dashboard, Hub access, onboarding items, welcome email
↓
3
Phase 0: Pre-launch -- Roster collected, staff provisioned, login verified, kickoff scheduled
↓
4
Phase 1-4: Active partnership -- Days 1-90 onboarding framework, ongoing Hub engagement, observation days, virtual sessions
↓
5
Renewal -- AI summary, engagement data, ROI proof, Year 2 vision conversation

2. Deal signed: the automation

When you mark a deal as Signed or Paid in the Sales pipeline, a "Create Partnership + Dashboard" button appears in the deal panel.

What the modal asks you to confirm

FieldPre-filled?Default

Contact name + emailYes (from deal)--
Partnership typeNoSchool
Staff count (Hub memberships)No0
On-site observation daysNo2
Virtual sessionsNo4
Executive sessionsNo2
Contract start/end datesNoToday / empty
Building count (districts)No1

What happens when you click "Create Partnership"

  1. Partnership record created in Leadership with all contract details
  2. Organization record created with school name, city, state, website
  3. Dashboard generated at /partners/[school-name-slug]
  4. 9 onboarding action items created (5 partner-visible, 4 admin-only)
  5. Hub All-Access provisioned for the contact email
  6. Welcome email sent to the principal from Rae
  7. Sales deal linked to partnership via sales_deal_id
  8. Activity logged with deal details

API: POST /api/admin/deal-to-partnership

UI: Sales deal panel footer (PanelFooter.tsx)

4. Leadership Dashboard (client-facing)

Every partnership gets a client-facing dashboard at /partners/[slug]. This is what the principal sees.

What the principal sees (Overview tab)

  • AI Summary -- one paragraph describing their team's engagement
  • 4 gauge rings -- Hub Engagement %, Deliverables, Team Wellness, Phase
  • Partnership Health bar -- Green/Amber/Red momentum status
  • Quick Actions -- Open Hub, Schedule, Quiz, Tools
  • Popular Tools + Educator Types -- what their team is using
  • Teacher quote -- anonymous quote from their building
  • Collapsible sections -- Hub Intelligence, Timeline, Data & Impact, Actions, Leadership Tools, Community & FAQ

Admin view (what you see at /tdi-admin/leadership/[id])

5. Internal tab (TDI team only)

The Internal tab on the partnership detail page is where your team documents everything about the school. Nothing here is ever visible to the principal.

Notes

  • Add notes with types: General Strategy Concern Win Follow up
  • Timestamped and attributed to the author
  • Can be promoted to partner-visible with the visible_to_partner flag

Meetings

  • Log meetings with: date, type, attendees, summary, action items
  • Meeting types: Check-in, Onboarding, Observation debrief, Renewal, Strategy, Escalation
  • Timestamped and attributed to who logged it

API: GET/POST/PATCH/DELETE /api/tdi-admin/leadership/[id]/notes

API: GET/POST/PATCH/DELETE /api/tdi-admin/leadership/[id]/meetings

Hub matching

Schools are matched between Hub and Leadership using fuzzy name matching on hub_profiles.school_name vs partnerships.org_name. This happens in /api/partnerships/[id]/hub-stats and /api/partnerships/[id]/hub-intelligence.

Wellness trigger emails

A daily cron (/api/cron/wellness-check, 10 AM CT) scans for educators with 2+ negative vibe scores (<=2/5) in the last 7 days and sends a personal check-in email from Rae. Names are never shared with the principal. The dashboard shows "TDI reached out to X team members."

7. Cross-portal navigation

From Leadership

  • "Sales" button on each partnership row -- jumps to Sales filtered by school name
  • "Dashboard" button -- opens the client-facing dashboard
  • "View" button -- opens admin detail page

From Sales

  • "Leadership" pill on deal panel -- jumps to Leadership filtered by school name
  • "Hub Data" pill on deal panel -- jumps to Hub admin filtered by school
  • "Create Partnership" button -- runs the deal-to-partnership automation

Both Sales and Leadership pages read ?search= URL parameters to pre-filter when arriving from cross-portal links.

Staff below target< 50% logged inDay 14Re-engage via champion
Champion disengaged0 logins in 7 daysAfter Day 7Direct call to champion
30-Day Report not viewed7 days after deliveryDay 37Direct principal call

Seasonal extensions: All principal-facing flag thresholds extend by 10 days during testing windows, winter break, first 2 weeks of school year, and spring break. Staff usage flags hold normal thresholds.

9. Renewal flow

Day 45: Plant the Year 2 vision

Not a renewal ask. A conversation about what growth looks like from here. Use the AI Summary + engagement data as talking points.

Day 80: Partnership continuation check-in

Confirm budget and discuss Year 2 priorities.

Day 90: Impact Report

Shareable with superintendent/board. Lead with one educator outcome, support with usage data. The Leadership Dashboard's AI Summary and gauge rings provide the data.

What you show in a renewal conversation

  • The client-facing Leadership Dashboard (Example Dashboard for demos)
  • The Loom walkthrough video (scripted June 2026)
  • The Hub itself (live demo of what teachers use)
