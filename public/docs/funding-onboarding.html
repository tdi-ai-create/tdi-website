Bella Owns all school contact. Calls, emails, follow-ups, nudges -- if it involves a school contact, Bella handles it. Manages the Work Queue daily. Requests agent drafts. Reviews narratives. Tracks every deadline. Escalates strategy or approval decisions to Rae.
