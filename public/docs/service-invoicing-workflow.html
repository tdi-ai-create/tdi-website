Service Delivery + Invoicing Workflow

How a signed contract becomes delivered services and paid invoices.

Last updated: July 14, 2026 | Created by Rae Hughart

The Big Picture

Contract Signed → Deliverables Created → Services Delivered → Invoice Sent → Payment Received

Every signed contract gets broken into individual deliverables -- one row per service (each observation day, each executive session, hub memberships as a batch, etc.). Each deliverable has a lifecycle status that tells you exactly where it stands.

Step-by-Step Workflow

Step 1: Contract is Signed

Rae closes the deal. Contract is created in the Contracts tab (Sales page) or via Anchor.

  • Each contract has line items: Hub memberships, observation days, executive sessions, virtual sessions, books
  • Line items are automatically broken into individual deliverables in the system
  • Direct-pay items start as Pending
  • Grant-funded items start as Awaiting Grant
Where to see it: Leadership Dashboard → [Client Name] → Billing tab → Service Deliverables section

Step 2: Grant Funding (if applicable)

Funding Team pursues grants for grant-funded deliverables.

  • Grant pursuits are tracked in the Funding dashboard
  • When a grant is awarded, the system automatically unlocks those deliverables from "Awaiting Grant" to "Pending"
  • The client's service totals on their dashboard update automatically

Step 3: Service is Delivered

Trainer / Rae delivers the service (observation day, executive session, etc.).

  • Go to Leadership Dashboard → [Client Name] → Service Tracker
  • Click the "Complete Session" button for the service type
  • Fill in: session date, notes, teacher quotes, love notes count
  • Click "Mark Complete"

When you mark complete, the system automatically:

  1. Creates a session record with all the details
  2. Updates the client's used/total service counts (client sees progress on their dashboard)
  3. Marks the matching contract deliverable as Delivered
  4. Adds a timeline event to the partnership history
  5. The deliverable appears in Bella's Invoice Queue
Important: Complimentary services (100% off) still get marked as delivered for tracking, but they do NOT appear in the Invoice Queue since there's nothing to bill.

Step 4: Invoice is Created

Bella creates and sends the invoice.

  • Go to Ops Dashboard → Invoice Queue
  • You'll see all delivered-but-not-invoiced services grouped by client
  • Each shows: service name, delivery date, dollar amount
  • Click "Create Invoice" to generate the invoice
  • The system auto-generates an invoice number (TDI-INV-2026-XXX)
  • Deliverables flip to Invoiced

Step 5: Payment Tracking

Bella tracks payment through the collections process.

  • When payment is received, mark the invoice as paid
  • Deliverables update to Paid

Two Types of Contracts

Direct Pay (Minimum Contract)

The client pays for these services directly. Invoice when delivered.

  • Shows with a green indicator in the Billing tab
  • Deliverables start as Pending
  • Follow the standard deliver → invoice → paid flow

Grant Funded

TDI pursues grant funding to cover these services. Only invoiceable when funding is confirmed.

  • Shows with a purple indicator in the Billing tab
  • Deliverables start as Awaiting Grant
  • Cannot be delivered or invoiced until the grant is confirmed
  • When the grant lands, the system automatically unlocks these services
Dual-contract clients: Saunemin, Glen Ellyn, St. Peter Chanel, and Allenwood all have BOTH a direct-pay contract AND a grant-funded contract. The Billing tab shows both, clearly separated.

FAQ

What if a service was delivered but I forgot to mark it complete?

Go to Leadership Dashboard → Service Tracker and mark it complete with the correct date. The system will find the next undelivered deliverable and update it.

What if a client doesn't want the grant-funded services anymore?

The deliverables can be marked as cancelled. This won't affect the base/direct-pay contract.

How do I know what's been invoiced vs. what hasn't?

Leadership Dashboard → Billing tab shows every deliverable with its current status. Or check Ops Dashboard → Invoice Queue for items ready to bill.

What if the contract value needs to change?

Talk to Rae. Contract changes go through the Contracts tab in Sales. The deliverables will need to be updated to match.

How does the client see their progress?

The Partner Dashboard (what the client logs into) shows service utilization: "2 of 3 observation days used." This updates automatically when sessions are marked complete -- no separate step needed.
