The founder
Rae Hughart — CEO and Founder
Former classroom teacher · TEDx speaker · Author · EdTech builder

Rae was a special education student told she wouldn't make it to college. At school she was invisible. Everywhere else - on the dance floor, at her father's work events, in any room she chose - she lit up. Same kid. Different system. That contrast never left her.

She became a teacher, then a director of training, then an author and TEDx speaker. She completed every goal she'd set for herself before 30 - and felt empty, because the ceiling the system had put on her had become her own. She looked around and saw the same pattern in the teachers she worked with: brilliant, capable people being quietly broken by a profession that was supposed to support them.

She didn't set out to build a company. She started helping teachers with what they were actually struggling with. It worked. It spread. Administrators asked for it at scale. TDI was pulled into existence by demand, not designed from a boardroom. That origin is the product's most important feature.

Why now
The Crisis
44% of teachers leave within 5 years
The pandemic broke something in the teaching profession that has not been fixed. Burnout, attrition, and morale are at decade lows. Districts are desperate for solutions that actually work — not more one-day workshops.
The Technology
AI enables what was impossible 3 years ago
Personalized PD tracking at scale, AI coaching agents available to every educator, and real-time implementation data that traditional PD companies cannot generate. TDI has built this stack. Competitors have not.
The Policy
Federal funding specifically for educator retention
Title II-A, ESSA workforce development provisions, and state-level educator retention grants have created a funding window that removes the primary purchase barrier for school districts. TDI's grant engine was built for exactly this landscape.

Mission and vision
Our Mission
Teachers Deserve It exists to build a system that actually supports the educators inside it - not by adding more to their plate, but by helping them work smarter, feel better, and remember why they chose this work in the first place.
Our Vision
A world where no great teacher leaves the classroom because of burnout. Where professional development is something educators look forward to - not dread. Where every school has the data, the support, and the systems to keep their best people.
TDI was born from the belief that teachers deserve better - not nicer words or a pizza party at the end of a hard week, but real, practical support that changes how they spend their time and energy. We put boots on the ground in your building and work with your whole staff - not just core teachers. Art teachers, coaches, paraprofessionals, everyone. Personalized support that reduces stress, improves retention, and actually gets used. 83% of teachers want additional support after a single PD session. TDI bridges the gap between inspiration and implementation - and because every plan is built new for every educator, every season, it never gets stale.
