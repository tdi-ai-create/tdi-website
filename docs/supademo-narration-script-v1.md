**Audience:** District/building leadership evaluating Hub access for staff; prospective individual members

**Voice:** AI for most slides. Slides 14, 15, and 20 recorded in Rae's voice from day one. Supademo supports per-slide audio upload.

## Production Notes

**Total slides:** 20  
**Estimated runtime:** 3-4 minutes at natural narration pace  
**AI voice tone:** Warm, steady, conversational. Not corporate. Not rushed. Think "trusted colleague walking you through something they believe in."

**Human voice slides (record first, ~10 min at mic):**
- Slide 14: "This is what makes the Hub different..." through "...they press one button."
- Slide 15: "A guided breathing exercise..." through "...we want you to stay in it."
- Slide 20: Full closing narration, especially "This is what supporting your people looks like."
- Upload these three as per-slide audio in Supademo. AI handles the other 17.

**Things to avoid during recording:**
- Don't click into a lesson (video player is placeholder)
- Don't click "Manage Subscription" (Stripe portal returns 501)
- Don't navigate to any admin/partnership pages (real PII in seed data)
- Don't show quick wins with "watch" format (video placeholder)
- Stay on the member-facing Hub only

**Demo account setup:**
- Seed data should already be loaded (run `node scripts/seed-example-data.js` if not)
- Ensure the demo account has: a streak >= 2, at least one favorited item, at least one certificate, and at least 2 check-ins (to unlock the transformation tracker)
- Confirm no real names or emails appear on any screen you'll record
