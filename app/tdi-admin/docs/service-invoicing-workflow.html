Service Delivery + Invoicing Workflow

From contract signing to payment received. Every step, every role, every system.

Last updated: July 21, 2026

The Pipeline

Contract Signed→ Service Scheduled→ Service Delivered→ Invoice Sent→ Follow Up→ Payment Received

Key Rule: Invoices are sent immediately after each service is delivered. Do not batch invoices. Do not wait until the end of the contract. One service = one invoice.

Step by Step

Step 1: Service is Delivered

Who: The person who delivered the service (Rae, team member)

Where: Leadership Dashboard → [School Name] → Billing tab

Action: Find the service line item and click "Mark Delivered"

This records:

  • The delivery date (defaults to today)
  • Who delivered it
  • Changes status from Pending to Delivered

Do this the same day the service happens. Don't let it pile up.

Step 2: Send Invoice (One Click)

Who: Bella or Omar (anyone with admin access)

Where: Same Billing tab, on the delivered service

Action: Click "Send Invoice"

This automatically:

  • Generates invoice number (format: TDI-YYMM-NNN)
  • Emails the invoice to the school's primary contact
  • Sets a 30-day payment due date
  • Changes status from Delivered to Invoiced
  • Creates a collections workflow record for tracking

The email includes: invoice number, service description, date of service, amount due, payment instructions, and TDI contact info. Reply-to goes to Rae.

Step 3: Follow Up on Payment

Who: Bella (primary), Omar (oversight)

Where: Billing tab → expand the invoiced service

Actions available:

  • Add a note: Record follow-up calls, emails, AP contact info
  • View timeline: See when the invoice was sent, any notes added
  • Check due date: Overdue items are flagged in red

Follow-up cadence:

  • Day 14: If not paid, send a friendly email reminder
  • Day 30: Due date. If not paid, call the AP contact directly
  • Day 45: Escalate to principal contact if AP is unresponsive
  • Day 60: Escalate to Rae for direct outreach

Step 4: Record Payment

Who: Bella or Omar

Where: Billing tab → click "Record Payment" on the invoiced service

Enter:

  • Amount received
  • Payment method (check, ACH, wire, credit card)
  • Check number (if applicable)
  • Notes (optional)

This automatically:

  • Changes status to Paid
  • Logs the payment event with all details
  • Updates the collections workflow

Status Flow

Pending→ Delivered→ Invoiced→ Paid

Special statuses: Awaiting Grant for grant-funded services. Overdue auto-flags when past due date.

Billing Tab Overview

The Billing tab on each partnership's detail page shows:

  • Summary cards: Contract value, paid, invoiced (unpaid), ready to invoice
  • Alert banner: Shows when services are delivered but not yet invoiced
  • Service list: Every contract line item with current status and action buttons
  • Expandable details: Click any service to see invoice number, due date, timeline, notes

Complimentary Services

Some services are marked as complimentary (e.g., bonus observation days, promotional sessions). These:

  • Show in the deliverables list with a purple "Complimentary" label
  • Cannot be invoiced (the "Send Invoice" button does not appear)
  • Do not count toward the contract value total
  • Still need to be marked as delivered for tracking purposes
